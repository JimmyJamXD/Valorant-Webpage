Valorant

We are Valorant

Defy the limits

Incorporate styles with abilities that agents have in a 13 round match up. Players will either attack or defend on the maps, creating and formulating environment for gunplay and usage of abilities to win the match. Take on teams in different game modes either in Normal, Competitive or Spike Rush.

    Role: Initator

    Initiators are the supportive class of agents in Valorant who - unsurprisingly - are particularly well suited to initiating fights and helping your team win them. These agents typically have abilities that allow them to provide information to the team, or disrupt the enemy.

    Examples: Sova, Breach, Skye, KAY/O, Fade

    Role: Controller

    Controllers in Valorant are all about using their smokes. These agents are able to block off certain areas of the map to make offensive or defense easier.

    Examples: Brimstone, Viper, Omen, Astra

    Role: Sentinel

    Sentinels are the defensive class of agents in Valorant. These agents typically slow down, stall, or sometimes outright stop enemy pushes on certain areas of the map.

    Examples: Killjoy, Cypher, Sage, Chamber

    Role: Duelist

    Duelist are the offensive class of agents in Valorant. These agents excel in one-vs-one fights and their goal is to break through the enemie's defense and win their duels

    Examples: Phoenix, Jett, Reyna and Yoru
